About Clean Wave

Founded in 2024, Clean Wave is a Zimbabwean manufacturer dedicated to producing high-quality cleaning products for households and businesses.

We started with three core products - dishwashing liquid, toilet cleaner, and liquid soap - with plans to expand our range to include laundry detergents, surface cleaners, and specialized industrial cleaning solutions in the near future.

Our mission is to provide effective, affordable, and environmentally conscious cleaning solutions that make everyday chores easier and more efficient.
